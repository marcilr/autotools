Appendix C. Further Readings
Autotools Mythbuster
Has nice list of books that I list below.
https://autotools.io/furtherreadings.html#bib.book.calcote

[AutoconfManual] GNU autoconf manual
http://www.gnu.org/software/autoconf/manual/html_node/index.html

[AutomakeManual] GNU automake manual
http://www.gnu.org/software/automake/manual/html_node/index.html

[LibtoolManual] GNU libtool manual
http://www.gnu.org/software/libtool/manual/html_node/index.html

[CalcoteAutotools] Autotools. A Practical Guide To GNU Autoconf,
Automake, And Libtool. John Calcote. No Starch Press. July 2010.
Looks good with:
o Master the Autotools build system to maximize your software's portability
o Generate Autoconf configuration scripts to simplify the compilation process
o Produce portable makefiles with Automake
o Build cross-platform software libraries with Libtool
o Write your own Autoconf macros
price: Ebook (PDF, Mobi, and ePub), $35.95
https://www.nostarch.com/autotools.htm

[AutotoolsBestPractices] Best Practices With Autotools
Diego E. "Flameeyes" Pettenò. August 2005.
http://www.linux.com/archive/articles/114061

[GentooAutomagic] Automagic dependencies, what they are and
how to fix them
Diego E. "Flameeyes" Pettenò. et al. .
http://www.gentoo.org/proj/en/qa/automagic.xml

[GentooAsNeeded] --as-needed introduction and fixing guide
Diego E. "Flameeyes" Pettenò. et al. .
http://www.gentoo.org/proj/en/qa/asneeded.xml

[MillerRecursiveMake] Recursive Make Considered Harmful
Peter Miller. 1997.
http://miller.emu.id.au/pmiller/books/rmch/
